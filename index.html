Who We Are

A Facade Engineering And Curtain Wall Design-Build Contractor


Zenith’s aim is to offer facade systems combining aesthetic, performance and economic considerations into optimum solutions to our client’s needs.


As a new player in the market, Zenith’s customer-focus services has become our company’s success by securing projects through referrals and repeat customers. The reputation has allowed us working on innovative projects, collaborative partnering with developers and construction companies.


Operations in facade industry requires massive documentation and coordination among clients, suppliers and installation teams. We streamline the Company Operation Procedure, and successfully accredited with ISO 9001:2015, ISO14001:2015, ISO45001:2018 and Integrated Management Systems for the provision of project management , including design, supply, installation and construction management.
