About Us

Founder - Anthony Tam

"Zenith's designers use their specialist skills to produce world-class resilts. We believe that high-quality products and services are the cornerstone to building a relationship with our clients."


Anthony has over 30 years of solid construction experience for major international construction projects in Hong Kong, Macau, UAE and Southwestern Pacific practicing as a successful project team leader. Anthony has overseen and been involved in numerous projects of various types and different magnitudes that include Infrastructure, Foundation and Geotechnical, Building Construction, ABWF & FitOut, Building Envelope and prestigious architectural facade projects designed by famous international’s Architect. Apart from Hong Kong, Anthony has established a good relationship and alliances that form with different field and areas of expertise and material suppliers from PRC, Macao and UAE and the network that Anthony created is deemed as one of the most important and valuable things for construction business development. Anthony has also specialized in fast track façade project with great success both in Macao, UAE and Papua New Guinea. Recently, Anthony has diversified his key role to manage all activities related to company operations and business development for Hong Kong, Macau, Union of Myanmar and the Middle East Region. In the Year 2016, he starts up his own business on building envelop industry to serve Hong Kong and Myanmar Region. In 2018, he was nominated as the Honorary President of HKSRSARO+.
