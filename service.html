Our Service

Our service focus on façade solutions by providing design-engineering capability, supplying the adequate façade materials with installation for the façade systems including curtain wall, glass wall, metal cladding, luxury retail storefront and metal roofing in the time frame and budget set. In other words, we provide turn-key designbuild façade contracting services to the market. For complex facade requiring the expertise on latest 3D software, our strategic partners both local and overseas collaborate to provide seamless engineering with wire framed 3D modeling and optimization.

Zenith – Health and Safety at Work Zenith’s priority is always the health and safety of our customers, employees, subcontractors, and suppliers. We have clear plans and processes in place so we can safely manage our building projects. For every individual project, we will assess every aspect of our site activities and address the hazardous situation, as well as establish a safe working system to fully comply with the safety requirements. Zenith PMS will adopt a policy of “promoting health and safety at work to create a safe and healthier workplace which is one pillar of our Integrated Management System, including ISO 45001 certification. Our commitment to Occupational Health and Safety has the highest priority which is as important as the other Company’s objectives such as Quality, Environmental Promotion, Productivity, as well as Economical Success. We are sure that all risks arising from work can be managed by a foresighted and careful behavior.
