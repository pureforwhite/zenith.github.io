Modular Integrated Construction (MIC)

Facade Construction is a challenging industry especially on the project site which logistics, weather and safety are factors that will influence the delivery process and quality. Over the years, facade systems have evolved from knockdown system to fully-unitized system that help reducing the site activities thus the improvements on quality controls, logistics and safety. With the Modular Integrated Construction (MiC), the facade system such as curtain wall, cladding and features can take another step with a great portion to be completed in the factory allowing better control on installation and water test can be tested and rectified before shipments. Zenith has embraced this new construction method and had successfully delivered projects in the recent years. Our experience allowing our team extend our services with total solution on delivering modular unit to construction site either urban or remote area.
